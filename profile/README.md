## What We Build

Veloqit is an AI-first SaaS company building intelligent operational software for India's service economy. Our products replace manual, WhatsApp-based workflows with smart automation — helping salon owners, travel agencies, and security teams run leaner and grow faster.

---

## Products

### 🌸 Veloqit Bloom — Salon Management SaaS
> *AI-powered operations for Indian salon owners*

WhatsApp-native booking automation, appointment management, staff gamification, predictive customer re-engagement, GST billing, and analytics — built for salons that run on WhatsApp today.

- **WhatsApp AI Booking Bot** — customers book via natural language in WhatsApp
- **Predictor Engine** — auto-detects when a customer is due for a visit and sends personalised offers
- **Gamified Stylist Dashboard** — real-time earnings leaderboard and milestone badges for staff
- **Deposit Collection** — Razorpay payment links sent inside WhatsApp chat

🔗 [bloom.veloqit.com](https://bloom.veloqit.com)
